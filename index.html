About Kerry

Financial planner at heart. Educator in practice.

Kerry Rizzo is a Chartered Investment Manager and CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER professional accredited by FP Canada, with more than 22 years of experience as an Investment Advisor.

Kerry Knows Money is the education side of her work: approachable conversations, grounded habits, and practical ways to help women feel more confident with money.
